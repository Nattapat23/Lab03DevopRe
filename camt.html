CAMT

CAMT (College of Arts, Media and Technology) is an autonomous college under Chiang Mai University (CMU) (วิทยาลัยศิลปะ สื่อ และเทคโนโลยี มหาวิทยาลัยเชียงใหม่), focusing on developing IT and digital skills for Northern Thailand's tourism, software, and handicraft industries, offering programs in Digital Games, Software Engineering, Animation & Visual Effects, etc., and can be contacted via their website (camt.cmu.ac.th), phone (+66 53 920 299), or email (prcamt@camt.info).
